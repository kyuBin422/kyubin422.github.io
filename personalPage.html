Bio

    Welocome! I am the first year Ph.D. sudent in Institute of Operations Research and Analytics at National University of Singapore . I am also affiliated with the SIA-NUS Corp Lab. I specialize in Stable Matching in Airline area. Fortunately, I am being supervised by professor Jussi Keppo. I earned my M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst and my B.Eng. in Industrial Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China
